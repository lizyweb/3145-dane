Development Association For Needy Elavation (DANE)

Development Association for Needy Elevation (DANE) is a non-governmental, social service organisation formed by educated people of the deprived community. Its development activities are centered around the downtrodden and oppressed people of backward villages of Tiruvannamalai District, Tamil Nadu.

About Us

Development Association For Needy Elavation (DANE)

Development Association for Needy Elevation (DANE) is a non-governmental, social service organisation formed by educated people of the deprived community. Its development activities are centered around the downtrodden and oppressed people of backward villages of Tiruvannamalai District, Tamil Nadu.

Our DANE adheres to strong governance practices to ensure accountability, transparency, and effectiveness. We maintain a structured Monitoring Committee that provides oversight and strategic direction while separating governance from daily management.

Objectives

Empowering communities through education, healthcare, and sustainable development, fostering self-reliance and improving quality of life for all.

Child Development

DANE recognise that each child has an inherent right to life.  And the society has the duty towards the development of the child’s personality, talents and mental, physical abilities to their fullest potential.

Women Development

Women’s contribution for development form a major share but they have little resources to call their own, They are deprived and denied of their rights to life, development, protection and participation.

Farmers Association

The small and marginal farmers are faced with multitude of problems. The efforts to equip them with knowledge and skills on rotation of crops, multiple crops, water management and working out the feasibilities of cooperative farming are being taken up.


Cultural Programme

DANE have a sound cultural team comprising of 10 volunteers.  The team goes to village to village for building awareness among the people on area-specific issues and problems through means of folk media.

Technical Education For Youth

DANE excels other institutions by giving efficient Technical Education for the school dropouts, poor and needy young boys and girls.  It has well versed resource personnel to teach the interested candidates on servicing house-hold electrical items and also audio and video equipment.

Linking People With Services

Most of the rural people are non-literates and unaware of the welfare measures meant for them.  DANE as a facilitating body, strives to link the disadvantaged people with services.

Our Programmes

Able Pre Primary School

Able Pre Primary School is a nurturing and dynamic educational institution dedicated to laying a strong foundation for young learners. Our focus is on fostering curiosity, creativity, and a love for learning in children aged 2 to 6 years.Through a blend of play-based learning, engaging activities, and age-appropriate curriculum, we ensure holistic development that covers cognitive, emotional, social, and physical growth.

DANE Children’s Club

DANE Children’s Club is a vibrant and inclusive platform designed to inspire creativity, build skills, and foster friendships among children. We offer a variety of engaging activities, including arts and crafts, storytelling, games, and cultural programs, all tailored to encourage self-expression and teamwork.

Electronics and Electrical Training Centre

DANE Electronics and Electrical Training Centre is a premier institution dedicated to empowering individuals with practical skills and technical expertise in the fields of electronics and electrical engineering. Our comprehensive training programs are designed to meet industry demands, blending hands-on workshops with in-depth theoretical knowledge.

Micro Credit Program for Women

Micro Credit Program for Women is an initiative aimed at empowering women by providing small-scale financial assistance to support their entrepreneurial ventures and livelihood activities. This program helps women gain access to affordable loans without the burden of excessive interest rates, enabling them to start or expand businesses, manage household needs, or invest in skill development.

Small & Marginal Farmers Association

Small & Marginal Farmers Association is a collective initiative aimed at empowering small and marginal farmers by addressing their unique challenges and fostering sustainable agricultural practices. The association provides a platform for farmers to access essential resources such as modern farming techniques, affordable credit, quality seeds, and market linkages to ensure better productivity and profitability.

Small Business Men’s Association

Small Business Men’s Association is a dedicated platform that brings together small-scale entrepreneurs to foster collaboration, growth, and sustainability in their businesses. The association provides support through networking opportunities, skill development programs, financial guidance, and access to resources that help address the challenges faced by small business owners.

Adult Education Programs

Adult Education Programs are designed to empower individuals by providing them with opportunities to learn, grow, and improve their skills, regardless of age or background. These programs focus on essential areas such as literacy, numeracy, vocational training, and life skills, helping adults achieve personal and professional development.

Health Awareness Programs

Health Awareness Programs are initiatives aimed at educating individuals and communities about the importance of maintaining good health and preventing illnesses. These programs focus on promoting healthy lifestyles, early detection of diseases, and access to essential healthcare services. Topics often include nutrition, physical activity, mental well-being, hygiene, and preventive care.

Tailoring Training Centre

Tailoring Training Centre is a dedicated space designed to empower individuals, especially women, with the skills and knowledge required to excel in the art of tailoring. Our training programs cover essential techniques such as stitching, cutting, pattern-making, and embroidery, catering to beginners and those looking to enhance their expertise.
